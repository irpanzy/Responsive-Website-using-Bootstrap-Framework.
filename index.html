WHERE PASSION, STORIES, AND CRAFTMANSHIP COLLIDE

The Compass® brand was established in 1998 in the city of Bandung, Indonesia. It was founded by Mr. Kahar Gunawan who wanted to rebuild a shoe factory based upon the vision left by his father, Mr. Kahar Setiadi. 2

The Compass® ethos has always been to produce good quality footwear that evoke a sense of pride when worn. Through a strong focus in design and storytelling, Compass® shoes tells the story of its culture, community and collaborators.

In 2018, the Compass® brand was reimagined with a fresh contemporary touch, while continuing to uphold its historical roots through the Gazelle® identity.

THE SPIRIT OF GAZELLE® LOGO

The logo created by Mr. Kahar Setiadi in 1983, was inspired by the gazelle, an animal known for its swiftness and agility. He wished to symbolize this spirit in a product that will have great enduring quality - just like a gazelle in full flight. 41 years later, the Gazelle® logo continues to be an intrinsic a part of Compass® DNA.

As the Creative Director of Compass®, Aji Handoko Purbo, is a leading force in the Indonesia's footwear industry. Aji has contributed to the success story of Compass® brand, making this local label one of the Top of Mind, cult brand in the nation. Today, Aji has proven himself to be a well-respected and one of the most admired personas in Indonesia's youth culture movement.
